Version 1 (July 2026) - Initial Release

- Implemented automatic viewport tracking for newly constructed stations, depots (all transport types), and vehicles across all active companies.
- Added the client_id setting to dictate which player or spectator camera is controlled by the script.
- Added the scroll_cooldown_days setting to enforce a configurable delay between camera movements, preventing erratic screen jumping.
- Added individual toggle settings (scroll_on_station, scroll_on_depot, scroll_on_vehicle) to allow users to enable or disable tracking for specific infrastructure types.
- Implemented background scanning loops with automatic state-saving to compare existing game entities against new additions.
